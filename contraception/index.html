Contraception

Learn to chart your menstrual cycle to know the fertile and infertile days and therefore avoid an unwanted pregnancy.

On average, each consultation lasts 1h30.

A complete course is done over 15 cycles, that is to say over a year, with one consultation every three cycles. It is only after 15 correctly analyzed cycles that you can consider yourself autonomous (and reduce your fertility window safely).

You will learn the basics of fertility awareness and cycle charting, learn about special cases (which always happen), how to regulate a cycle, how to manage the fertile phase, how to include your partner and how to prepare for menopause.

For more information, please see the main website.
